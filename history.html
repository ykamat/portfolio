History of The Marvel Cinematic Universe

Phase 1: The Beginning

The first phase of the MCU introduced popular comic book characters.

Phase 2: The Expansion

The second phase mostly focused on developing the current roster of characters, while introducing a few new ones.

Phase 3: The Universe

The third phase of the MCU, their biggest thus far, linking characters from different franchises in Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame.

Phase 4: The Future

The fourth phase looks to move on from the Infinity Saga and focuses on building new superheroes.
